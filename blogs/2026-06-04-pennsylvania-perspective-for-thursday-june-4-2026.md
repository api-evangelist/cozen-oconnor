Pennsylvania lawmakers face a $3.65 billion structural deficit while negotiating the state budget ahead of the June 30 deadline. Governor Shapiro's proposal calls for a $4.5 billion transfer from the Rainy Day Fund and new revenue sources through marijuana legalization and skill-games taxation. The competing priorities of closing the structural gap and funding new initiatives are shaping intense legislative negotiations in Harrisburg.
