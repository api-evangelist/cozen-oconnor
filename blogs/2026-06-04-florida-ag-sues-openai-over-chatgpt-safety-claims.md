Florida Attorney General James Uthmeier filed the first state-led lawsuit against OpenAI and CEO Sam Altman, alleging the company marketed ChatGPT as safe while concealing risks including self-harm, violence, and addiction. The complaint accuses OpenAI of prioritizing commercial growth over user safety and collecting data from children under 13 without proper parental consent. The state seeks damages, restitution, civil penalties, and an injunction.
