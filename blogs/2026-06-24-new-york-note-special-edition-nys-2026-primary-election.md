The 2026 New York State primary elections were held yesterday, June 23. All of the candidates endorsed by Mayor Zohran Mamdani won their primary elections, reinforcing the strength of the Mayor’s political capital and the Democratic Socialists of America. Notably, many of these candidates unseated long-time incumbents.
