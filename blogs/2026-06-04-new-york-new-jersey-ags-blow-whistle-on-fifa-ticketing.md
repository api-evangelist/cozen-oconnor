New York AG Letitia James and New Jersey AG Jennifer Davenport subpoenaed FIFA to investigate ticketing practices for the 2026 World Cup, centered on allegations that fans were deceived regarding seat locations after FIFA introduced premium "Front Categories" following initial sales. Investigators are also examining variable pricing strategies and ticket-release schedules, noting that FIFA increased prices for over 90 of 104 matches between October 2025 and April 2026. The subpoenas signal expanding state enforcement scrutiny of major international sporting event ticket markets.
