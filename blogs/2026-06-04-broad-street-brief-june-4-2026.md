City Council rejected Mayor Parker's proposed taxes on rideshare and delivery services while advancing a $7.1 billion budget. The council approved a plan to purchase the Police Department headquarters with $200 million in bonds and advanced legislation extending death benefits to families of first responders who die by suicide. Philadelphia's budget deliberations highlight ongoing tensions between revenue generation and public safety investment priorities.
