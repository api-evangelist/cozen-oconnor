A recent case in the 7th Circuit, upholding the criminal convictions of two supervisors based on their falsification of health and safety records, serves as an important reminder that although uncommon, the Occupational Safety and Health Act does provide for criminal penalties. The underlying OSHA inspection stemmed from an explosion that resulted in multiple fatalities. […]
